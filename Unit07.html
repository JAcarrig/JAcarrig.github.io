Unit 07 is a game jam project made for Metroidvania Month 20. I worked with 4 others on the project, and I was the programmer and team lead. From this project, I gained a lot more experience in coordinating and leading a game project, and knowing what kind of documentation and organization such a role entails. On the technical end, I gained a lot of reusable code I can put towards future projects.

The player takes the role of the titular Unit 07, a prototype combat android who wakes up one day to discover she has been granted an sense of empathy. Realizing the untold destruction that will be unleashed should she and her sisters make it to production, 07 resolves to destroy them, and the factory producing them.
