BATTLE BLIMPS

INFORMATION

50 Player Battle Royale where players control a blimp equipped with artillery cannons. The cannon's rotation is controlled by clicking a point on the screen, but the angle (and thus the range) must be controlled by a slider next to the player's blimp, requiring skill in judging distance, or firing multiple shots.
I worked with 1 other programmer on this project, and was responsible gameplay elements including the cannon aiming and firing mechanics, and wind affecting cannonballs in flight.
